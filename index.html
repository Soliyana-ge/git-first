CUP CAKE

Welcome to the CUP CAKE page!

Enjoy our delicious cupcakes made with love.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Baking powder
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add butter, eggs, milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into cupcake liners in a muffin tin.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cool before serving.
